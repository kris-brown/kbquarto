
Write the 2x2 identity matrices for each of the quantales:

1. $(\mathbb{N},\leq,1,*)$
2. $\mathbf{Bool}:=(\mathbb{B},\leq,true,\land)$
3. $\mathbf{Cost}:=([0,\infty],\leq,0,+)$

::: {.callout-note collapse=true appearance="minimal"}
## Solution

1.

| 1 | 0 |
|--|--|
| 0 | 1 |

2.

| T | F |
|--|--|
| F | T |

3.

| 0        | $\infty$ |
|--|--|
| $\infty$ | 0        |


:::
